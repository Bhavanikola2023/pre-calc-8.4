Vector Operations and Dot Product

1. Sine of Sum

sin(α + β) = sinα·cosβ + cosα·sinβ

2. Sine of Difference

sin(α - β) = sinα·cosβ - cosα·sinβ

3. Cosine of Sum

cos(α + β) = cosα·cosβ - sinα·sinβ

4. Cosine of Difference

cos(α - β) = cosα·cosβ + sinα·sinβ

5. Tangent of Sum

tan(α + β) = (tanα + tanβ) / (1 - tanα·tanβ)

6. Tangent of Difference

tan(α - β) = (tanα - tanβ) / (1 + tanα·tanβ)
